Ingredients

  • Chicken, raw

Steps

  1. Go to shop or send Ma to shop
  2. Look for refridgerated meat section
  3. Find packet of refridgerated breast fillet chicken
  4. Purchase packet of chicken
  5. Go home
  6. Once home, wash your hands after being outside in a public shop where god knows who touched what. Go to the toilet if you need to. Have a little rest but DON'T HAVE A NAP otherwise you'll have such a difficult time falling asleep later that night.
  7. Once rested, heat a non-stick frypan
  8. While frypan is heating, cut chicken breasts into large pieces approximately 5cm long,
  9. Fry chicke. Do not add any oil. Fat from chicken is sufficient.
  10. Fry until chicken is cooked through. Cut a piece in half to determine whether there are any pink bits.
  11. Once cooked, let cool. And then place in refridgerator.
  12. The next day or up to 3 days later, take chicken out of the fridge and select about 3-4 pieces. Cut into 1-2cm chunks and place into Whiskey's favourite bowl
  13. Heat in the microwave, covered, for 20 seconds. Once finished, check to ensure the chicken is warm, but cool enough to heat without burning.
  14. Call Whiskey over and serve
